    US allies reject Trump’s call for help in the Strait of Hormuz


    US President Donald Trump has called on allies to help protect shipping in the Strait of Hormuz, a key oil transit route, after Iran seized a British tanker. The UK has deployed a warship to the area and the US is sending an aircraft carrier strike group. Iran says it will not back down and warns of consequences if the US takes action.
